/History || /H:

Tests if the current song is in history, if it is in history and you are permitted to skip it, this
command will also skip the song.

/Skip || /S:

This command will skip the current song if you are permitted to do so.

/Userlist || /U:

Toggles whether the userlist is hidden or shown.

/Fix userlist || /UU:

Refreshes the userlist.

/Auto woot || /AW:

Toggles the auto woot feature.

/Auto join || /AQ:

Toggles the auto join feature.

/History alerts || /HA:

Toggles the history alerts feature.

/Share history alerts || /SHA:

Toggles whether or not you send a message over the chat when the song playing is in the
history.

/Hide || /HV:

Toggles whether the video is hidden or shown.

/Settings || /set:

Shows the settings window.

/My song || /MS:

Tests whether the song you are about to play is in the rooms history or not.

/Boo:

Mehs the current song.

/Curate messages || /CM:

Toggles whether you get messages in the chat when someone curates the song or not.

/RSB:

Removes the settings button completely.
(Note: The only way to undo this is to refresh the page)
